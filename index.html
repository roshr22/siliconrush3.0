About

Confluence

Confluence is the IEEE Computer Society's upcoming 24 hours hackathon this February. The hackathon is a chance for coders to unleash their creativity, innovate and test the limits of their skills and knowledge. If spending 24 hours coming up with ingenious ideas and create effective solutions for real-world problems sounds like the perfect way to spend your weekend, this is just the hackathon for you! Teams will be selected based on their problem statements, so think outside the box and give us your best!
At the end of the 24 hour hackathon, teams will be deploying their app and presenting it to the judges!

When and Where?

  • Dates - 14th and 15th February 2025
  • Venue - 13th Floor, BE Block, PES University RR Campus
  • Time - 04:00 PM - 06:30 PM

Solve Real-World Problems

Themes

Presentation Template
 1.   AI for a Smarter, Greener Planet

Imagine a world where technology works hand in hand with nature to create a sustainable future. This theme focuses on using AI to tackle climate change, protect forests, and optimize renewable energy systems for cleaner living. It’s about rethinking waste through smarter management, embracing circular economies, and empowering farmers with AI-driven tools for sustainable agriculture and water conservation. Together, we can build a greener, more harmonious planet for generations to come.

Example tracks:
• AI for climate change mitigation (e.g., carbon tracking, forest monitoring)
• Smart grids and renewable energy optimization
• AI for waste management and circular economies

 2.   The Future of Mobility: AI in Transportation

Reimagine how we move, connect, and explore. This theme envisions a world where AI makes transportation safer, faster, and more sustainable. From autonomous vehicles and AI-powered navigation to reducing traffic and preventing accidents, technology transforms the way we travel. It also inspires smarter logistics, seamless deliveries, and greener solutions like electric and hydrogen-powered vehicles—all designed to create a more human-centered and connected future.

Example tracks:
• Autonomous driving and AI-powered navigation systems
• AI for urban traffic optimization and accident prevention
• Intelligent logistics and supply chain optimization
• AI in electric and hydrogen-powered vehicles

 3.   AI for Human Well-Being

At its heart, this theme is about using AI to care for people and improve their lives. Imagine AI-powered healthcare tools that save lives with early diagnoses, or mental health solutions that provide timely support to those in need. Picture technologies that make the world more accessible for people with disabilities and tools that ensure safer, healthier workplaces. This theme is a call to create solutions that uplift humanity and prioritize well-being for all.

Example tracks:
• AI-powered healthcare diagnostics and wearable devices
• Mental health solutions using AI for early detection and support
• AI for accessible technologies (e.g., tools for people with disabilities)
• Enhancing workplace safety using AI-driven monitoring

 4.   AI-Powered Future Cities

Imagine living in cities that truly understand and adapt to our needs. AI-powered future cities bring smarter urban planning, traffic systems that ease our daily commutes, and energy-efficient buildings that reduce our carbon footprint. From predicting natural disasters to enabling faster emergency responses, AI ensures our cities are not just smart but safe. By blending technology with community care, these cities promise a sustainable, inclusive, and vibrant future for everyone.

Example tracks:
• AI in urban planning and traffic systems
• Smart building technologies with energy-efficient designs
• AI for disaster prediction and emergency response
• Urban safety: AI-driven surveillance and crime prediction systems

 5.   Revolutionizing Education with AI

Education shapes lives, and with AI, it can become more inclusive, engaging, and impactful. Picture personalized learning platforms that adapt to each student's pace and style, or immersive virtual reality classrooms that bring lessons to life. AI tutors can help bridge educational gaps in underserved communities, while intelligent tools automate assessments, freeing up teachers to focus on what matters most—nurturing young minds. This theme inspires innovations that make education accessible and empowering for all.

Example tracks:
• AI for personalized and adaptive learning platforms
• Virtual reality and gamification for immersive education experiences
• AI tutors for skill development in underserved communities

 6.   AI for Entertainment, Media, and Gaming

Imagine stories that evolve as you play or music composed just for you. This theme dives into the exciting world of creativity fueled by AI—where generative tools craft stunning art, dynamic games, and immersive experiences in virtual reality. From enhancing how we stream and interact with media to creating personalized entertainment, AI unlocks endless possibilities for storytelling, creativity, and fun. It’s where innovation meets imagination to captivate and inspire.

Example tracks:
• AI for generative art, music, and storytelling
• AI in game development (e.g., NPC behavior, dynamic storytelling)
• Enhancing user experience in streaming and media platforms with AI
• AI for immersive experiences in AR/VR

 7.   AI in Crisis Management and Disaster Relief

In moments of crisis, every second counts. AI has the power to save lives by predicting disasters in real time, guiding search-and-rescue teams, and ensuring humanitarian aid reaches those who need it most. It can revolutionize pandemic prevention, optimize resource allocation, and provide tools for communities to rebuild stronger. This theme is about using technology to bring hope, compassion, and life-saving solutions when humanity needs them most.

Example tracks:
• AI for real-time disaster prediction and resource allocation
• Search-and-rescue robots powered by AI
• AI for monitoring and managing humanitarian logistics
• Predictive analytics for pandemic prevention and control

 8.   The Boundless Potential of Generative AI

Generative AI is unlocking new realms of creativity and innovation. From designing virtual assistants that simplify our lives to creating breathtaking art, videos, and music, this technology is reshaping how we work and imagine. It’s revolutionizing industries like design, manufacturing, and science, solving complex problems like protein folding and accelerating breakthroughs. This theme is an invitation to explore how AI can unleash human potential and reimagine what is possible.

Example tracks:
• AI for creating virtual assistants and customer service bots
• Generative AI for design, architecture, and manufacturing
• AI in content creation: Text, images, and video generation
• Generative AI for solving complex scientific problems (e.g., protein folding)


Do note, the example tracks given per theme are not strictly to be followed; you are welcome to explore and choose any topic or track that resonates with you!

FAQs

Looking for answers?

When and where is the Hackathon going to be held?

February 14th and 15th 2025, 13th Floor BE Block, PES University RR Campus

Is there a registration fee for the Hackathon?

Selected Teams will have to pay a registration fee of ₹200 per member.

What should I bring?

You'll need to bring your own laptop, chargers, any external hard drives or hardware you plan to use, and most importantly - your enthusiasm and creative spirit! No hardware will be provided from IEEE CS PESU's side. While we encourage you to bring snacks and beverages to keep your energy up, please note that our venue has a strict no-tolerance policy for drugs, alcohol, cigarettes (including e-cigarettes), lighters, or any harmful substances. Anyone found in possession of these items will face immediate disciplinary action. Consider bringing a small cushion or sweater to stay comfortable.

Do I need a team? What is the team size for the hackathon?

Yes, this is a team event. A team may consist of 3-4 members.

Do I need to have any prerequisites? Do I require any skills?

None! While experience in coding and programming is a huge plus, your idea will carry you through the hackathon. Your final product can be built using any language and any open-source APIs.

Why should I participate in this hackathon?

This hackathon will give you exposure, teach you about new tech stack and will help you network and connect with peers. You will be given the chance to build résumé-worthy projects and interact with industry experts. Not to mention the eye-catching cash prize.

Is the event open to everyone?

Absolutely! Students of all branches and years are welcome to participate.

How many rounds do I have to go through?

There will be a preliminary round before the hackathon where participants will be asked to submit their ideas. We will pick 30 teams and they will be expected to make a working model of their idea on the day of the Hackthon.
